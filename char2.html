Henri – The Exiled Warbringer



Role: Bruiser / Tank
Shard Affinity: Iron & Fury



Core Abilities:


  • Crushing Leap – Slams into an area with earth-shattering force,
                              stunning foes.

  • Shardbound Armor – Activates shard-powered gauntlets to absorb
                                      incoming attacks and reflect a portion back.

  • Rage Unbroken (Ultimate) – Temporarily becomes unkillable for 6 
                                                 seconds. Gains damage buff per enemy
                                                 nearby.


Playstyle: Frontline disruptor, crowd control, survives impossible odds.
