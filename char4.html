Hein – The Ancient Sentinel



Role: Support / Controller
Shard Affinity: Stone & Memory



Core Abilities:


  • Guardian’s Anchor – Roots enemies in place while projecting a
                                     protective dome around allies.

  • Remnant Strike – Slams the ground to summon spectral warriors
                               from past battles.

  • Last Stand (Ultimate) – Creates a stasis field where no one can die 
                                         for 8 seconds. Allies heal slowly inside.


Playstyle: Area control, defense, team support through ancient magic.
